How to start?


You must be already signed up to log in.

 



When you log in for the first time, you will see the dashboard. Inside, you will have access to the sessions list of the courses you are enrolled. 

In the middle, you will see your profile image and your name. This can be changed in the profile settings section.


At the top right corner, there is a button with your profile image. Upon press, a menu page will swipe in. Inside, a few option buttons will appear. 






This is the menu , where you will see a menu with a few buttons like profile settings , app settings , tutorial and a log out button. 



Common users can view the resources of their subjects, but only teachers can create new resources.




If you press the resource you will see the downloadable files with a brief description.
